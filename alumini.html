IMSEC Alumni Network

Welcome to the IMSEC Alumni Network, a platform for connecting and celebrating the former students and faculty members of IMS Engineering College, a NAAC accredited with 'A' grade engineering college in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, India. Here you can find the latest news, events, achievements, and testimonials of our alumni, and also register or update your contact details, share your feedback, suggestions, or queries, and subscribe to our newsletter or magazine. Join us and be a part of our vibrant and growing community!
